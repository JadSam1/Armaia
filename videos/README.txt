# Adding Real Videos to the "For You" Page

To add real videos to the JadSam "For You" page, follow these instructions:

## Video File Requirements

1. **Format:** Use MP4 format for maximum browser compatibility
2. **Resolution:** Recommended 1080x1920 pixels (9:16 aspect ratio)
3. **Duration:** Keep videos between 15-60 seconds for optimal performance
4. **Size:** Compress videos to under 10MB each for faster loading
5. **Content:** Fashion-related content that matches the JadSam aesthetic

## Steps to Add Videos

1. Name your video files consistently:
   - videos/fashion-video-1.mp4
   - videos/fashion-video-2.mp4
   - videos/fashion-video-3.mp4

2. Place the video files in the "videos" directory

3. Add corresponding thumbnail images:
   - images/video-thumbnail-1.jpg
   - images/video-thumbnail-2.jpg
   - images/video-thumbnail-3.jpg

4. Ensure thumbnails match the first frame or a representative frame of each video

## Free Stock Video Resources

If you don't have your own fashion videos, you can use free stock videos from:

1. **Pexels:** https://www.pexels.com/videos/
2. **Pixabay:** https://pixabay.com/videos/
3. **Coverr:** https://coverr.co/

Search for fashion, clothing, models, or shopping terms to find relevant content.

## Video Compression Tools

To optimize your videos for web:

1. **Online:** 
   - https://www.freeconvert.com/video-compressor
   - https://clipchamp.com/en/video-compressor/

2. **Desktop:**
   - HandBrake (free, all platforms): https://handbrake.fr/
   - FFmpeg (command line): https://ffmpeg.org/

## Testing

After adding your videos:
1. Open for-you.html in a browser
2. Videos should autoplay when scrolled into view
3. Click on a video to toggle sound
4. Test in both Grid and Phone views
